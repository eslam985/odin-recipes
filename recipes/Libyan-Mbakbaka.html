Libyan Mbakbaka

Libyan Mbekbeka is a unique pasta dish that combines pasta and a rich, spiced tomato sauce in one pot. The dish is characterized by being "mbekbeka," meaning the pasta is cooked directly with the sauce and chicken, giving it a rich and satisfying texture. It's considered a quick and easy main course.

the components

  • Chicken breasts
  • Large onion
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 3 cloves of minced garlic
  • Tomato juice
  • one tablespoon of tomato puree
  • macaroni
  • Salt, black pepper, turmeric, and chicken seasoning

Preparation method

  1. Cut the onion into small pieces and put it in oil on the fire and stir until it wilts a little, then add the garlic and stir, then the chicken pieces and stir, then the chopped green pepper and spices.
  2. Mix all the ingredients well, then add the tomato juice and leave it on medium heat until the chicken pieces are almost cooked. Then add the pasta and boiling water, covering the pasta by 3 cm.
