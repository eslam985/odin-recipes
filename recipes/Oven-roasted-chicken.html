Oven-roasted chicken

Oven-roasted chicken is a healthy and delicious option, where the chicken is marinated in a mixture of vegetables and spices for a robust flavor and a crispy exterior. This method ensures the chicken remains moist and retains its internal juices, making it suitable for large gatherings.

the components

  • 10 chicken
  • 2 kg grated onions
  • 2 kg grated tomatoes
  • 10 chopped chili peppers
  • 10 cloves of minced garlic
  • 3 tablespoons sauce
  • A quarter cup of oil
  • 3 tablespoons of spices
  • 3 tablespoons of salt
  • 3 tablespoons of pepper
  • A spoonful of bay leaf on ground cardamom
  • one spoonful of ground nutmeg
  • A quarter kilo of flour
  • 2 squeezed lemons

Preparation method

  1. Blend all the vegetables and spices together in the blender, but the mixture will be coarsely chopped.
  2. We put the mixture on the chicken and leave it for a while, maybe an hour or more, maybe overnight.
  3. Remove the excess marinade before putting it in the oven.
  4. We turn on the oven on high heat and put an empty tray to heat up and put the chicken on the tray from time to time, turning the chicken over and that’s it. It doesn’t take long. After half an hour, we can find it cooked. Please do not leave it for a long time so that it does not become dry and retains its juice.
